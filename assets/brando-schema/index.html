Brando® V1.1 – The World’s Leading Brand Definition Framework

Brando® (short for Brand Oracle) is a modern brand definition framework designed to help organisations build AI-powered brand platforms. It makes it easier to keep your brand consistent, fine-tune your messaging, and simplify business operations. By using advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) and the latest machine learning techniques, Brando® provides a flexible and organised way to create AI-driven messages, solve problems, automate business document creation, and gain valuable insights. This toolkit offers a smart, data-focused solution to today's branding challenges.
