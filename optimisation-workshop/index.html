    AI Optimisation Workshop

    Overview

    The AI Optimisation Workshop is a strategic, hands-on 4-day engagement designed to help your organisation align brand strategy, data infrastructure, and operations with AI-driven opportunities.

    Over the course of 3–4 weeks, our consultants work closely with your team to assess your AI current state, identify high-value opportunities, and develop a tailored implementation roadmap.

    Objectives

    • Evaluate how AI-ready your brand, website, and operations are
    • Identify AI opportunities aligned to strategic goals
    • Create a machine-readable brand governance layer
    • Build a clear roadmap for deploying intelligent systems responsibly
    • Ensure internal alignment and leadership support for AI adoption

    Key Outcomes

    By the end of the workshop, you'll have:

    • A clear, actionable AI Optimisation Plan
    • An audit of brand, data, and tech structures for AI compatibility
    • Prioritised AI use cases and automation opportunities
    • A stakeholder-aligned roadmap with governance principles
    • Confidence in measuring impact and ROI (AIOps strategy)

    Workshop Format

    Week Focus Session(s) Duration
    Week 1 Discover Kickoff + Day 1 Monday (3 hours)
    Week 2 Assess & Design Day 2 (Mon), Day 3 (Fri) ~2–3 hours each
    Week 3 Roadmap Day 4 + Alignment Friday (3 hours)

    Total Client Time Commitment: ~11–13 hours over 3 weeks
    Total Consultant Time Commitment: ~5.5–6 days including prep

    Workshop Breakdown

    Day 1: Discover & Define

    • Review brand guidelines, web architecture, and structured data
    • Identify strategic priorities and AI opportunity zones
    • Evaluate content and tone-of-voice readiness for AI
    • Understand stakeholder vision and pain points

    Day 2: Assess & Plan

    • Perform AI maturity and process gap analysis
    • Audit structured data and accessibility for machine readability
    • Assess AI tooling, talent, and leadership readiness
    • Draft strategic AI optimisation blueprint

    Day 3: Solutions & Use Cases

    • Map AI-powered workflows (e.g. agents, assistants, automation)
    • Explore Brando-compliant schema and policy layers
    • Prioritise high-impact, low-risk use cases
    • Align on pilots and experiments

    Day 4: Roadmap & Governance

    • Deliver final AI roadmap with milestones
    • Define governance model, policies, and oversight frameworks
    • Present leadership alignment pack
    • Provide optional implementation support plan

    Key Questions Answered

    • How can AI support your brand promise and goals?
    • Are your digital assets structured for intelligent systems?
    • Do your policies support responsible AI use?
    • Is your team aligned on value, risk, and impact of AI?
    • How will you measure success and ROI from AI efforts?

    Investment

    • Consultant Day Rate: £1,250 – £2,000
    • Total Workshop Cost: £5,000 – £8,000 (4 days delivery + prep)

    Who Should Attend

    • CMOs / Brand Leaders
    • CTOs / Heads of Data or Digital
    • CX / UX / Innovation Managers
    • Policy & Governance Leads
    • AI and Automation Product Owners

    Optional Add-ons

    • AI Readiness Audit Report (PDF)
    • Post-workshop Implementation Sprint
    • Brando Schema Mapping and Integration
    • AI Agent Deployment Support
