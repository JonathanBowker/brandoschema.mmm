    The Schema for Building AI-First Brands

    Transform your website into an AI-native brand platform

    Brando® - short for Brand Oracle, is a Schema.org compatible Brand Definition Vocabulary (BDV).

    It overlays static brand guidelines, governance policies and digital assets with an intelligence layer for transforming them into machine-readable data.

    This enables you to build flexible brand knowledge graphs for the AI-powered web, ensuring consistent discovery, representation, and control across AI systems, LLM interactions, and intelligent search.

    Core Concept: brando:BrandObject

    At the core of the Brando Schema is brando:BrandObject — the foundational node in your brand knowledge graph, designed to encode the Six Pillars of Brand Identity for AI:

    • Verbal Identity—Tone, vocabulary, style, and prompt scaffolds.
    • Visual Identity—Logo, colour palette, layout, and typography.
    • Audio Identity—Voice persona, intonation, sound cues, and audio UX.
    • Brand Positioning—Mission, promise, audience personas, and market context.
    • Brand Governance—Rules, escalation paths, constraints, and ethical guardrails.
    • Brand Assets—Files, templates, schemas, and Q&A source materials.

    What You Can Do With BDV

    • Embed your brand knowledge graph in your communications workflows.
    • Drive dynamic assistant behaviour and AI prompt construction.
    • Serve as a machine-readable brand book for internal or external agents.
    • Feed BDV into Brando Audit tools, CMS back-ends, or vector indexes.
    • Enable persona-switching and multi-modal tone control (text, image, audio).

    Agentic AI Workflows

    • Configure agent personas — Dynamically load brando:BrandObject settings to initialize tone, style, and behaviour.
    • Policy-driven decisioning — Embed brando:Governance rules into planning chains to enforce guardrails and compliance.
    • Multi-agent orchestration — Spin up specialised sub-agents (e.g., voice-formatting or brand-consistent copywriting) based on each Identity pillar.
    • Prompt template scaffolding — Inject brando:PromptScaffold into chain-of-thought prompts to ensure brand aligned LLM interactions.
    • Contextual memory indexing — Index conversation state or facts against brando:BrandAssets for instant retrieval.
    • Dynamic persona switching — Switch between audience personas (brando:AudiencePersona) or tone profiles at runtime.
    • Audit & feedback loops — Route outputs through Brando Audit tools, feeding back compliance tags and visibility ratings.

    Why Brands Need Brando

    As AI-powered systems and LLMs dominate customer interaction and content gatekeeping, brands face new risks:

    • Misrepresentation in LLM-driven dialogues
    • Loss of tone, intent, or distinctiveness in AI-generated content
    • Compliance, ethical, or reputational failures in automated outputs
    • Invisibility in the semantic web and conversational search

    Traditional SEO is no longer enough. Brands must optimise for AI with machine-readable knowledge graphs and control policies that govern brand representation at inference time.

    The Case for AI-Native Brand Governance

    The AI web demands that brands become AI-native entities capable of interaction, reasoning, and responding reliably across platforms, plugins, and voice agents.

    Without clear brando: policies on who you are, what you believe, and how you behave, brands risk losing visibility, control, and trust.

    Brando bridges your static guidelines and the systems interpreting them, creating a semantic chain from policy to real-time output.

    What Brando Does

    Brando transforms brand assets into machine-actionable controls, enabling:

    • Brand-accurate LLM responses
    • AI-safe usage of visual assets and tone
    • Structured metadata for search and plugins
    • Governed agent interactions
    • Fully compliant and ethically aware outputs

    Your brand becomes:

    • Discoverable by intelligent agents
    • Interpretable by LLMs and APIs
    • Governable in real time
    • Consistent across all AI use cases

    Brando as Operating Infrastructure

    Brando makes your brand:

    • Machine-readable
    • AI-visible
    • Compliance-aware

    Brando is the first schema that lets brands declare who they are, what they believe, and how they behave on the AI-powered web—in a format AI can enforce.
